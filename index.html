Unity · 3D Roguelite · Binding of Isaac–inspired

Stack the items,
break the cannon.

A 3D twin-stick shooter inspired by The Binding of Isaac. Every upgrade is a self-contained modular effect on your cannon — more projectiles and bigger and homing and sticky, all at once. Because each item touches one parameter, they stack freely with no special-case code.

modular upgrades
One item, one parameter, infinite combos.
🥊

Boxing glove

Fires one more projectile per shot. Stack it to fill the room.

🍄

Mushroom

Scales your projectiles up — bigger balls, bigger hits.

🪖

Magneto's helmet

Projectiles steer themselves toward the enemy mid-flight.

🧴

Glue stick

Projectiles stick to whatever they hit instead of bouncing off.

the cannon
Composable shot parameters.

The cannon holds its shot state as independent fields — any combination is valid simultaneously. Items (or dev hotkeys) flip them on.

nn           projectiles per shot
ss           projectile size scale
homo_mode    homing toward the target
stick_mode   stick to surfaces on impact
bounce_mode  bounce instead of dying on hit
turret_mode  fired balls fire their own balls
shooting_mode  straight shot vs. ballistic arc
float_ball   hang in the air, no gravity
the world
Procedurally generated dungeons.
🧩

BSP rooms

Levels are carved by recursive binary space partitioning, then connected up.

🌊

Wave Function Collapse

Tile-based generation fills out the layout from a small ruleset.

👹

Enemies & boss

A multi-state boss wanders, chases and attacks; oil droplets hazard the floor.

🎮
